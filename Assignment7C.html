About the Game

The game was originally intended to be a casual party game. However the game mechanics allowed players to move around much faster than the game developers had intended and because of this, the competitive scene was born.

Things like wavedashing, L-Canceling, and short-hops all allowed the player to move around much quicker. Some maneuvers require you to be "frame-perfect". On average a Melee player will press three to six buttons per second while moving around and attacking their opponent.

Because of the massive skill ceiling, Super Smash Bros. Melee is widely considered the most competitive game in the series.
